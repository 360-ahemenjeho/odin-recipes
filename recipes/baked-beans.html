Baked Beans

Description

This easy baked beans recipe with bacon is my favorite. Mustard, brown sugar, and ketchup add a delicious flavor to the beans.

Ingredients

  • ½ pound bacon, chopped
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 (15 ounce) cans baked beans
  • ¼ cup brown sugar
  • ¼ cup ketchup
  • ¼ cup prepared mustard, or to taste

Steps

  1. Place bacon in a large, deep skillet. Cook over medium high heat until evenly brown. Add the onion and sauté until tender. Drain excess oil, if desired.
  2. Stir in beans, brown sugar, ketchup, and mustard. Cook, stirring occasionally, until bubbly.
